Медианный score участников по типу задачи
SQL: SELECT tt.code,
       percentile_cont(0.5) WITHIN GROUP (ORDER BY lr.score) AS median_score
FROM task_type tt JOIN competition c ON tt.task_type_id = c.task_type_id JOIN participation p ON c.competition_id = p.competition_id JOIN leaderboard_row lr ON p.participation_id = lr.participation_id GROUP BY tt.code;
